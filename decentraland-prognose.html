What is Decentraland?

Decentraland is a decentralized virtual social platform that is built, governed, and owned by its users¹. It is powered by the Ethereum blockchain, which enables users to create, explore, and trade digital assets and experiences in a persistent online world².

How does Decentraland work?

Decentraland consists of two main components: LAND and Worlds. LAND is a scarce digital asset that represents a parcel of virtual space in the open world of Decentraland. Users can buy, sell, and rent LAND using the MANA cryptocurrency, which is also used to pay for goods and services in the platform¹. Worlds are personal spaces that users can create and customize with their own NAME, separate from the open world of Decentraland. Users can build, experiment, host events, and invite friends to their Worlds using the Decentraland SDK³.
